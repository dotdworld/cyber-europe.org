Cyber Threat Intelligence & Internet Research

Cyber Europe is an independent cybersecurity research project focused on internet measurement, cyber threat intelligence, threat hunting and open-source security research. The platform is being developed to collect publicly available internet metadata, analyse emerging threats and provide practical intelligence for defenders.

Learn More

Current Focus

Cyber Threat Intelligence

Building an independent intelligence platform by combining open-source feeds, public telemetry and original research.

Internet Sensors

Developing custom sensors capable of collecting publicly available network, protocol and certificate metadata for security analysis.

Research Projects

Creating practical cybersecurity tools, dashboards and educational resources for researchers and defenders.

Project Status

Cyber Europe is currently under active development. The first public release will include custom internet sensors, threat intelligence feeds, research articles and open-source projects related to cybersecurity. Additional services, dashboards and APIs will be introduced progressively as the platform evolves.
